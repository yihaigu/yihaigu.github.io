Nov 14th, 2017

Daily News:

The failure of Segwit 2x confirm Bitcoin's status as digital gold. There are 2 parties that fought fiercely against to each other for many years. One is to support the original chain and wants to increase the blocksize only to 2MB. They believe that Bitcoin is a way to store the value, like precious medals rather than a payment. The other is to support Bitcoin become a payment first, for example, Bitcoin Cash, which leads by Jiang Zhuoer, the CEO of a Chinese Miner company.
